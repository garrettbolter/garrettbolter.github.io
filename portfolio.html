The inside scoop on how I stay busy

The Daily Tar Heel


As a content developer at the local student-run newspaper, I have seen the product of my work in real time. Wordpress edits I make for The Daily Tar Heel's website enhance UX/UI. Marketing flyers I design are stapled across campus. Client website renovations are making steady progress. From concept to polished product, my voice (and code) rings in the ears of the Carolina public.

Freelance Digital Marketing


Around the time final exams came to an end for my first year at UNC, a lightbulb clicked in my brain. Taking into account my graphic design skills, I devised a plan to consult storefront businesses in digital marketing. Utilizing the Snapchat interface, my strategy drives organic sales by generating foot-traffic via promotions and aesthetic geofilters. To date, I have generated over $1000 revenue, driving over 110K target-market impressions for my clients.
